🗣️ Spoken Languages in Bihar

Bihar is rich in cultural heritage, and its languages reflect that richness. Here are the main languages spoken across the state:

  • 💬 Bhojpuri
  • 💬 Maithili
  • 💬 Magahi
  • 💬 Angika
  • 💬 Hindi (official)

✨ Example Sentences in Local Languages

Here’s how people say “How are you?” in different Bihari languages:

  • Bhojpuri: का हाल बा?
  • Maithili: अहाँ केना छी?
  • Magahi: तू केसन ह?
  • Angika: तोहर की हाल बा?
  • Hindi: आप कैसे हैं?
